Satellaview+ is the revival service to St. Giga's sattelite service, Satellaview, which allows you to play special Satellaview games, read magazines, and more!


　The Satellaview+ service works on emulator and is planned to work on retail console in the future. The connection setup is down below on how to set up the connection for your emulator.

Satellaview+ Broadcasting Hours
　8am - 11pm (US EST)
　Please get more information of the programs from the Program Schedule
　Also, if there is a special event, it will be listed in the Special Events webpage.
